India Design 2023 ID

Ad.

Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

Each February, India Design ID, an initiative of Ogaan Media and the country's premier luxury design event, is held at New Delhi's expansive NSIC Grounds in Okhla. It is the biggest exhibition of the very finest in furniture, architecture, and interior design. The India Design ID Exhibit, which took place over five exciting days, from February 22 to February 26, 2023, featured expertly curated displays and iconic collections of furniture, lighting, interiors, kitchens, accessories, flooring, and home technology from well-known local and international brands.

One of ID's most well-liked attractions this year was the thoughtfully organised Symposium programme with its aim to support and promote a love of design through presentations, panel discussions, interactive sessions, debates and dialogues. Among the notable speakers were Aman Nath, co-founder of Neemrana Hotels and author; Amit Syngle, MD and CEO of Asian Paints group; Arthur Mamou-Mani, Head of his own architectural business in London; Ashwini Desphande of Elephant Design; Manuela Gatto of Zaha Hadid Architects; and numerous others. ID excels in creating a language that combines Indian and global aesthetics. Given the presence of numerous reputable domestic and international brands at the display, it was one of the finest chances for exhibitors to interact, discuss, and explore design interventions and architectural concepts all under one roof. ID included established brands such as Oorja, Lladró, Jaipur Rugs, Asian Paints, Stonex; but also introduced new exhibitions and pavilions such as Hunar, Living Traditions, ID Symposium and ID Collectibles to encourage indigenous artists and brands, women ceramic artists, emerging and experimental designers who are shaping the architectural and interior design practices across India.

The Head of Strategy and Curator of India Design ID who also curated the first ever ID Collectibles Pavilion, Misha Bains, perfectly described ID 2023 in the following manner, “This year's upcoming INDIA DESIGN ID 2023 presents global brands, concepts and renowned names from the field of interiors and architecture. Spearheading trends and charting new paths, ID 2023 speakers are award-winners, the top in their field from across the globe. This 11th edition is programmed to spotlight the best in design, craft and techniques from India and across the globe.”

Studio Smitamoksh

Studio Smitamoksh is an art and design studio founded by dynamic artist duo Smita Thapar and Moksh Thapar. They have embraced a distinctive approach to art, with special emphasis on producing works that heal and strengthen the natural environment. In order to investigate the innate knowledge of tradition, arts, and crafts that have developed through the continual interaction of human life with nature, the studio works with artisans and designers situated in Sanand, Ahmedabad.

Ghar Ghar

Ghar Ghar Textile Company is a unique home décor company that is focused on making home styling fun and playful. The company takes inspiration from childhood memories of playing house or "Ghar Ghar" as it is known in India. The company understands that our homes should be a space where we can express ourselves and have fun, just like we did when we were kids. With this in mind, Ghar Ghar has created a range of products that allow customers to play with their home décor and turn styling into playtime.

Veaves

Based in Banaras (Varanasi), a city with a rich weaving tradition passed down through generations, Veaves is a brand with profound roots in Indian culture and heritage. Its goal is to bring back the craft of hand weaving, celebrate its historical significance, and introduce it into ordinary daily life. With the aim of introducing high-end, ethical apparel to the market, the company caters to Indian consumers. They aim to create a community that appreciates traditional Indian craft blended with modern taste. They strive to nurture and expand a school of thought that celebrates design, quality over quantity, and responsible living. The brand hopes to strengthen the connection between the weaver and the consumer, acting as a platform for the craft to be seen.

One O One Studios

One O One Studios is a furniture and product design company founded by Yasha Mehta and Hiren Ganatra. They aim to bring unique and one-of-a-kind statement pieces to life, infusing character and exuberance into any room. Unlike normative, commercial design styles, One O One Studios produces original, urban designs made in India. Their maiden collection, Swell, named after a medley of waves, offers a splash of design in its modern eclectic style.

Tulio

Tulio, the brainchild of Raajkumarri Mutha, is a brand that is synonymous with beauty, culture, and tradition. Tulio has been making bespoke curtains and upholstery for more than three decades. These products are not only aesthetically beautiful, but also deeply embedded in India's rich cultural tradition. Their commitment to excellence has made them the brand of choice for architects and interior designers worldwide.

Escape

The brand Escape by Creatomy represents the fusion of traditional workmanship and modern design. In order to produce high-quality, cost-effective lifestyle and home décor products, Prateek Sabharwal launched Escape Studios in 2014. They are well-known in the hospitality, retail, and residential sectors thanks to their creative design method.

Material Immaterial Studio

The Material Immaterial design studio was founded out of a love for creating artistic products with careful attention to detail and a focus on striking a balance between form and function. Their design ethos is based on the idea of emphasising the natural beauty of materials. Because each of their handcrafted products is individually constructed, no two items from their studio are ever exactly alike in terms of texture and appearance.

Intent Made

Intent Made is a furniture design studio that combines contemporary design with traditional craftsmanship to create unique, sustainable, and functional pieces of furniture. The studio offers a range of furniture, including seating, tables, storage, and lighting, that exude simplicity and elegance. Their designs are characterised by clean lines, minimalist aesthetics, and functionality, which seamlessly blends with any interior decor. With a focus on custom design and craftsmanship, the studio caters to individual preferences and designs furniture that is tailor-made for each client.

The Carpet Cellar

The Carpet Cellar specializes in rugs and carpets, offering a diverse range of antique, vintage, and custom-made textiles, including kilims, durries, shawls, and more. Patrons from all around the world are drawn to their collection because of its originality and elegance. The Carpet Cellar's team possesses expertise in wool, cashmere, and weaving. They consistently seek for the best quality raw materials and work with traditionally skilled artisans to produce stunning pieces that perfectly capture the essence of art in transition.

Diviana

Since the company's founding in 2004, Diviana has been a frontrunner in luxury furniture, specialising in the design of customised living spaces. Kapil Chopra, who harbours a deep love for fashion, product, and interior design, created the company. The brand has grown into a household name in the luxury furniture field and has a reputation for producing stunning, enduring pieces.

Emery Studio

Emery studio represents the most desirable and sought-after names in bespoke decorative lighting, tableware, and home décor solutions and boasts an excellent range of top-tier international luxury brands, from Saint-Louis to Daum. In order to ensure that each product is expertly handcrafted using the finest resources and methods, the studio works with notable architects and interior designers who deliver refined and elegant designs, custom-made for their diverse clientele.

White Domus

White Domus, a specialized art and design studio, was founded in 2016 by brothers Aroosh and Nitush Mahipal. They aim to revolutionize the use of stainless steel in interior design by incorporating modern art into everyday objects using cutting-edge technology and skilled craftsmanship. The brothers draw inspiration from nature's fundamental beauty and familiarity, which they breathe into metallic forms and embellish into larger-than-life sculptures.

Hands

Hands Carpets has a remarkable legacy spanning over 140 years, during which they have honed their craft to create some of the world's most exquisite and high-quality carpets with intricate and innovative designs. The company has become renowned for its ability to produce bespoke, handcrafted carpets that can be found in some of the most exclusive hotels, palaces, and residences around the world, cementing its position as a leader in the luxury carpet industry.

Asian Paints ColourNext

Asian Paints ColourNext is an exhibition of innovative colour and material forecast that showcases the emerging trends, design ideas, colours, and textures that will influence the design landscape in 2023.

Oorja

The concept behind Oorja is to transform trash and natural materials into beautiful lighting fixtures that also serve as a constant reminder of the essential need to build a more sustainable future. The company handcrafts lamps for retail usage.

Lladró

Lladró, the Spanish porcelain company, has been applying its unmatched expertise in porcelain for over 60 years. At the India Design Week, Lladró displayed its most innovative and cutting-edge porcelain, lighting, and timely classic masterpieces combining design with age-old craft techniques as an expression.

Indie Eye Home

Indie Eye Home is an initiative by Sunil Sethi Design Alliance which delves into creative designs for a variety of home decor projects. Their focus is on engaging with the varied interests of their clientele and curate products that suit their diverse individuality and eclecticism.

Stonex

Stonex India is a prominent luxury brand and they are known for their high quality and exquisite selection of imported marble stones for facades, interiors, and floors. Stonex India is renowned for its superb craftsmanship,

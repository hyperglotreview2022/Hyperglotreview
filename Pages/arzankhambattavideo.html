A Ceramic Artist, Painter and Architect from Bombay, India - Adil Writer

Adil Writer (@adilwriter, @adilwriterartpage) is a ceramic artist, painter and architect from Bombay, India, currently living and working in Auroville universal township in south India. From his own studio at Mandala, he creates his own line of studio pottery. His Navagraha series is on view at the Art of India Exhibition till 13th April.

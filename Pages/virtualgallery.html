Dipankar Ray

The canvas for Dipankar Ray becomes a space where stories of the inner mindscape are brought to life. Inspired by nature, his artwork is a celebration of the smallest of creatures which is drawn in the most vivid of colours. While nature and its creations is the object of interest, rapid urbanisation is ever present in the background which raises the larger question of the position of man vis a vis the larger environment. Our innermost thoughts are only a manifestation of our experiences and journeys which in turn are affected by society at large. Ray's artistic vision is a revelation of all these thoughts.

Samir Sahar

Samir Saha was born in Kolkata, West Bengal, in 1958. He earned a BFA from the Indian College of Art & Draftsmanship, Kolkata, in 1983. He also took an art-appreciation course at the University of Kolkata and has participated in several art camps in India.

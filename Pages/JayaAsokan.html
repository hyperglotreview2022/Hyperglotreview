Redrawing Artscapes and Blurring Boundaries

Ad.

From 9 to 12 February 2023, the NSIC Exhibition Grounds in New Delhi will host the India Art Fair, the nation's leading platform for presenting modern and contemporary art from India and South Asia. The fair will feature 85 exhibitors, including 71 galleries and 14 institutes, partnering with BMW India and with the voices of artists at its core. With the India Art Fair in charge, the country's art sector has expanded in tandem with India's projected rise to the fifth-largest economy in the world. The 2023 fair, which will build on the success of the previous edition, will be the most ambitious to date, featuring expanded floorspace to display South Asia's top talent, including cutting-edge contemporary art and modern legends, and an extended Studio presenting the fair's Digital Artist In Residence Programme.

Jaya Asokan, Fair Director of India Art Fair has said that “The scale and diversity of the fair in 2023 reflects the expansion of the contemporary and modern art market across India and South Asia, as well as burgeoning interest from the international community. We’re particularly proud of all of our artists who are testing the boundaries of contemporary and traditional arts or digital innovation, with many coming from previously unexplored regions of India. We're proud of the quality of work they're producing and powerfully responding to our changing times.”

Some of India's most prominent art galleries will be participating at India Art Fair 2023, showcasing established and emerging artists as well as promising young artists.

Few of the top Indian contemporary galleries exhibiting include Gallery Espace, Nature Morte, Blueprint12 (all New Delhi), Chatterjee & Lal, Jhaveri Contemporary, GALLERY MASKARA, Galerie Isa, EMAMI ART, Gallery Art Exposure (both Kolkata), Experimenter (Kolkata / Mumbai), Gallery WHITE and Kalakriti Art Gallery (Hyderabad), each of them from across India and beyond the traditional centres of New Delhi and Mumbai. New gallery participants presenting emerging and mid-career artists include: 079 | STORIES ART GALLERY (Ahmedabad), Treasure Art Gallery (New Delhi), Gallery Dotwalk (Gurugram), Dhi Artspace (Hyderabad), Chemould CoLab (Mumbai), Iram Art (Ahmedabad).

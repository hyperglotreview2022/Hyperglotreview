Featured Artist

Amit Saha focuses in his paintings on human figures in general and transgender figures in particular. His works delve into and empathetically depict the complex emotions and embattled lives of the transgender subjects who, though historically an integral part of Indian society and culture, are seldom recognized as such and rather treated with ambivalence at best and rejection at worst.

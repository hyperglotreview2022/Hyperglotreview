Moti Zharotia

Ad.

Moti Zharotia is renowned for his expertise in Silk Screen Printing. With a passion for art that brings him inner peace, Zharotia beautifully captures the essence of life, nature, and spirituality in his artworks. His prints showcase a stunning array of colours, employing various tools and screens to create intricate and mesmerizing designs.

Zharotia's artistic journey began with his graduation in Graphics & Paintings from Delhi College of Arts, Delhi University in 1979. Since then, he has emerged as a visionary artist who views art as a celebration of life itself. His works have been featured in numerous group shows and camps on both national and international platforms, earning him recognition and praise.





Shows and Exhibition

Among his notable achievements, Zharotia participated in the 'Indian Contemporaries, Art Russia 2017' exhibition, the 'Kibriya Art Festival' in Bangladesh in 2017, and the 'Tokyo International Mini Triennial Tama Art University' in Japan. In 2018, his participation in the 'Festival of Print Making' show in Athens, Greece further solidified his reputation as an accomplished artist.





Awards and Acheivements

- Golden IKCOON Awards 2017
- Hues Exhibition by Kala Care Foundation and American Center, Delhi.
- He was invited to the first International 'Print Biennale Exhibition' organized by Lalit Kala Academy, New Delhi in 2018

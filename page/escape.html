The brand Escape by Creatomy represents the fusion of traditional workmanship and modern design. In order to produce high-quality, cost-effective lifestyle and home d√©cor products, Prateek Sabharwal launched Escape Studios in 2014. They are well-known in the hospitality, retail, and residential sectors thanks to their creative design method.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

At the 2023 India Design Week, Escape Studios debuted their latest collection, the Nautical Reef furniture line. The collection is inspired by the ocean and its captivating beauty and features nine distinctive furniture pieces that are designed to elevate the interior of any home with their sculptural quality and exceptional comfort.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

Among the standout pieces in the collection is the Seafoam Bookshelf. Its design takes inspiration from the gentle reef formations under the sea, and it is perfect for showcasing your treasured books and decor items. The Kelp Floor Lamp is another remarkable piece with its unique shape that mimics the natural movement of seaweed in the ocean, creating a tranquil ambiance in any room.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

The Clam Sofa is a centerpiece for any living room, offering style, comfort, and a touch of oceanic beauty. Its unique shape allows for lounging and relaxation with friends and family. The Driftwood console is a stunning sculptural piece that replicates the water's movement on its surface, with its legs inspired by coral formations in the ocean, making it an excellent choice as a foyer table.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

For sun-lovers, the Boa Two-Sided Lounger is a comfortable and stylish piece for any accent area. Its snake-inspired design offers ample space for two people to bask in the sun and is sure to spark intrigue and conversations in any space. Lastly, the Reef Dining Table, crafted from solid wood and glass, features a smooth surface and sturdy legs inspired by the coral reefs found in the ocean. Its organic shape makes it a striking focal point and ideal for entertaining guests.

The brand assists in expressing oneself beyond the mundane, and it produces the most extensive assortment of furniture that spans numerous styles while remaining faithful to its quality, variety, and price.

Diviana

Ad.

Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

Since the company's founding in 2004, Diviana has been a frontrunner in luxury furniture, specialising in the design of customised living spaces. Kapil Chopra, who harbours a deep love for fashion, product, and interior design, created the company. The brand has grown into a household name in the luxury furniture field and has a reputation for producing stunning, enduring pieces.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

One of the brand's standout collections, the Q Collection, was launched at the 2023 India Design Week, has an international appeal and is centred around the characteristics of modernistic and minimalistic elegance. The collection has a distinctive fusion of global flair and localised workmanship because it is designed in Italy and produced in India. The Q Collection is more than a collection of furniture; it is a "style of living" that can make any interior space a statement of contemporary luxury.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

Clean lines, a sense of proportion, and utmost attention to detail are what make up the Q Collection. It is the ideal representation of contemporary luxury, with soft shapes, textures, and hues that emanate sophistication and grace. It is an outstanding furniture collection with an international appeal that transforms any interior environment into a lifestyle statement.

Diviana believes that luxury is more than just being expressive; it is about invoking an experience that is intimately personal. The firm has experimented creatively with materials, techniques, designs, and finishes throughout the years, which puts them in a special position to meet the personalised home decor desires of their customers.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

Diviana has been featured in prestigious industry publications and has taken part in some of India's most prestigious design and home décor exhibitions. Diviana is raising the bar for high-end furniture manufacturers all over the world with its distinctive fusion of cutting-edge materials and finishes, imaginative space arrangements, and individualised aesthetic. They are renowned for making stunning, high-quality pieces that are built to last, and today their name is a byword for urban luxury and sophistication.

Janice Pariat

Ad.

Image Credit - Author

Janice Pariat is an acclaimed author known for her diverse literary contributions. With works like "Boats on Land: A Collection of Short Stories" and "Seahorse: A Novel," she has garnered recognition and received prestigious awards, including the Young Writer Award from the Sahitya Akademi and the Crossword Book Award for Fiction.

Pariat's writing transcends borders, as her bestselling novella "The Nine Chambered-Heart" has been published in multiple languages worldwide. Pariat has also received writing residencies and fellowships in the UK and South Korea, showcasing her global perspective and creative prowess. Her latest novel, "Everything the Light Touches," has received critical acclaim, winning the AutHer Award for Best Fiction and being recognized as one of The New Yorker's "Best Books of 2022."

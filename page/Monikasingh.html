Monika Singh

Ad.

Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

Monika Singh, the dedicated visual artist and art teacher, boasts an academic background in Science, a testament to her diverse interests and capabilities. Actively participating in national and international group art shows and workshops, she has left her mark on the global art scene. Notable exhibitions at India Habitat Centre and AIFACS, along with group shows in countries like Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Poland, and Nepal, have showcased her talent on an international stage. Monika's artistic journey has seen the spotlight of prestigious venues through five solo shows at India Habitat Centre, Lalit Kala, and Jawahar Kala Kendra.
Her preferred medium of expression, the pen, serves as a conduit for her signature scribbling technique, distinguishing her work with a unique style and allure. Through her art, Monika aims to capture the essence of nature and explore spiritual themes, often depicted through her captivating Krishna paintings. Her constant quest to expand her creative horizons and share her vision with the world exemplifies her unwavering dedication to her craft. Monika Singh's artistic prowess and commitment to her craft continue to inspire and captivate audiences worldwide, leaving an indelible mark on the art world.

46th International Kolkata Book Fair: Tue, Jan 31, 2023 - Sun, Feb 12, 2023

In the winter, Kolkata hosts the International Kolkata Book Fair (formerly known as the Calcutta Book Fair) . It is distinctive in that it is not a trade show; instead, it caters more to the general public than wholesale distributors. It is the biggest non-trade book fair in the world, the biggest in Asia, and the most popular. After the Frankfurt and London Book Fairs, it is the third-largest yearly gathering of books worldwide. Many residents of Kolkata view the book fair as being inextricably linked to the city, and it's not unusual for people to go every day while it's going on. The fair features picnickers, singer-songwriters, and sugar floss vendors, along with the usual carnival attractions with a literary twist. It is the largest book fair in the world in terms of attendance, with over 2 million people attending in total.

According to the Frankfurt Book Fair organisers, the popularity of the Kolkata Book Fair played a significant role in India's selection as the Guest of Honor at the Frankfurt Book Fair in 2006. Kolkata's literary, theatrical, musical, and limerick communities have all participated in the book fair celebration.

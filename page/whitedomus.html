White Domus

Ad.

Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

White Domus, a specialized art and design studio, was founded in 2016 by brothers Aroosh and Nitush Mahipal. They aim to revolutionize the use of stainless steel in interior design by incorporating modern art into everyday objects using cutting-edge technology and skilled craftsmanship. The brothers draw inspiration from nature's fundamental beauty and familiarity, which they breathe into metallic forms and embellish into larger-than-life sculptures.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

The company's name, White Domus, reflects their view of home décor as a white canvas with endless possibilities for artistic expression. Their approach involves creating unique statement pieces that interact with light and surroundings, offering viewers an immersive and interactive experience. They collaborate with leading interior designers and architects to ideate and experiment in design art. They have mastered steel and work with the best craftsmen to deliver the best quality art.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

At India Design, White Domus showcased their new collection of sculptural objects, furniture, and lighting, which are a masterpiece of science, creativity, and expert craftsmanship that creates a unique and timeless aesthetic. The pieces are both functional and beautiful, including the captivating bubbly floor light, sleek console, cozy pouf, eye-catching Crescent wall sculpture, and their signature Peter Rabbit. The Carambola sculpture, which comprises six pieces that come together to form a beautiful 180cm by 90cm floor sculpture with a subtle matte finish, was the centerpiece of their booth at India Design.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

White Domus believes that every space should be as unique as the people who inhabit it, and their sculptural objects are the perfect way to achieve that vision. Their collection features stunning pieces that are both functional and beautiful, including the Tronco Armchair, Granada coffee table, and abstract wall panel in stainless steel. Their unique and pioneering approach to interior design, their attention to detail and dedication to quality have made them a sought-after brand for those looking to elevate their home décor. With their "If you can dream it, you can do it" attitude, they transform homes into unique spaces for self-expression with experiential installations that use seemingly impossible reflections.

Pabitra Pal

Ad.

Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

Pabitra Pal is known for his exceptional talent in print-making. Pal completed his Bachelor's degree from 1999 to 2004 at Kala Bhavan, Vishwa Bharati University, Shantiniketan. Following his undergraduate studies, he pursued a Master of Fine Arts in Print-making from 2004 to 2006, further refining his techniques and artistic vision. Pal has exhibited his artwork in several notable group shows, notably at Lalit Kala Academy in 2012 and 2013.


Image Credit - Hyperglot Review

He was honored with the Certificate of Merit at the 67th and 68th All India Fine Arts Exhibition in Kolkata, and in 2002, he received the 74th State level A.I.F.A.C.S Award in Kolkata. He took part in the 48th National Exhibition of Art at Lalit Kala Academy and the International Trinal Print Exhibition in Turkey and Craco, Poland. With a passion for incorporating technology and contemporary aesthetics into his work, he has gained recognition and acclaim at both national and international exhibitions.
